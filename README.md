## General Controls

- Player 1:
	Up Arrow: Throttle / Accelerate
	Down Arrow: Brake
	Left Arrow: Turn Left
	Right Arrow: Turn Right
	
- Player 2:
	W Key: Throttle / Accelerate
	S Key: Brake
	A Key: Turn Left
	D Key: Turn Right
